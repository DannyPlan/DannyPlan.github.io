A Little Bit of Bio

  My name is Daniel Plante, born and raised a Minnesotan by Wisconsinites, I spent my grade school years in North Branch Minnesota before taking myself south of the twin cities to Minnesota State University of Mankato. This is where I achieved my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering. A couple of years in and I found a more specific interest in cybersecurity. The Information Security class offered at the university fed the craving but pushed me to spend some time in external sources of cybersecurity knowledge. I began a student worker position for Medtronic's CRM Product Security and Privacy team in 2021 and grew to love the team, role, and future possibilities.
